About Me

I am a passionate scientist specializing in quantum molecular dynamics, mathematical modeling, and high-performance computing simulations. With a focus on the quantum behavior of molecules, my work spans a broad spectrum of topics from molecular scattering to entanglement entropy.

Research Interests

I specialize in quantum molecular dynamics, mathematical modeling, algorithmic developments, and high-performance computing simulations of molecular systems. My work primarily focuses on understanding the fundamental processes at the molecular level, including the effects of temperature on molecular scattering and quantum aspects of molecular interactions.
